PMPML - Schedules, Routes and Stops

PMPML
PMPML is a public transportation provider in Pune which operates Bus routes.

The PMPML has 512 Bus routes in Pune with 7915 Bus stops.

Their Bus routes cover an area from the S.T.Stand Lonavala stop to the Varvand Gaon stop and from the Junnar (Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Putala) stop to the Sarola stop.

PMPML line schedules (timetables, itineraries, service hours), and departure and arrival times to stations are updated in the app in real time.
