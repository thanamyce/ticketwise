Services



Bus Ticket Booking:

TicketWise provides a seamless online booking platform for PMPML Pune buses, allowing users to easily search for available routes, select preferred departure times, and reserve seats with just a few clicks. By eliminating the need to visit physical ticket counters, TicketWise simplifies the booking process and saves commuters valuable time and effort.



Pass Reservation:

For frequent travelers, TicketWise offers the convenience of pass reservation, allowing users to secure passes for regular bus routes. With pass reservation, commuters can enjoy hassle-free travel without the need to purchase tickets for each journey individually. This feature is particularly beneficial for commuters with fixed travel routines, such as daily commuters or students.



Secure Payment Options:

To ensure a safe and secure booking experience, TicketWise offers multiple payment options for online transactions. Users can choose from various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, mobile wallets, and net banking, with robust security measures in place to protect their financial information. By prioritizing user security, TicketWise instills confidence and trust among its users.
